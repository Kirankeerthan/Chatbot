Parks and Waterparks

Parks and Waterparks are favorite places of children. The joy which they get is priceless.
Here are a few Parks and Waterparks in Hyderabad.

Botanical Gardens

Botanical Garden of Hyderabad is also one of the interesting sightseeing places in Hyderabad offering a refreshing setting and rich flora. Having been developed by the Forest Departments. Botanical Garden is situated in Madhapur near the Hi-tech City which is almost 16 km away from centre of the city.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SUNDAY: 5:30am-6:00pm

Nehru Zoological Park

Located at a distance of 16 km from Hyderabad.Nehru Zoological Park is one of the largest zoos of India and one of the top sightseeing places to visit in Hyderabad. Run by the Forest department, Government of Telangana, the zoo has been named after Jawaharlal Nehru, the first prime minister of the country.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SUNDAY: 8:00am-5:00pm

Directions: Click Here


KBR National Park

Kasu Brahmananda Reddy National Park is a national park located in Jubilee Hills and Banjara Hills in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The park has an approximate area of 390-acre (1.6 km2). The palace complex was given to Prince Mukarram Jah on his coronation by his father Prince Azam Jah in 1967. It was declared as a National park by the Andhra Pradesh state government after getting approval from the central government in the year 1998.

Directions: Click Here

NTR Gardens

NTR Gardens Hyderabad: Built in the memory of late Shri N T Rama Rao, former and one of the most popular chief ministers of Andhra Pradesh, NTR Memorial Gardens are one of the famous sightseeing places of Hyderabad. Spread across an area of 36 acres, NTR Gardens present a soothing atmosphere and refreshing setting to spend some time.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SATURDAY: 2:30Pm-8:00pm
SUNDAYS/HOLIDAYS: 12:30pm - 8:00pm

Directions: Click Here


Jalvihar Waterpark

Jalavihar Hyderabad: A popular amusement park, Jalavihar Hyderabad is situated on the Necklace Road which is in the heart of twin cities-Hyderabad and Secunderabad. It is quite close to the IMAX multiplex and Sanjeevaiah Park which is also on the Necklace Road. An entertaining place to be with family, Jalavihar Water Park in Hyderabad, presents innumerable rides to its guests.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SUNDAY: 11:00am-7:00pm

Directions: Click Here


Wonderla Amusement Park

Wonderla is the largest chain of amusement parks in India. It is owned and operated by Wonderla Holidays Limited which is headquartered near Bidadi, 28 kilometres. Wonderla amusement parks offer a variety of dry rides such as roller coasters, ferris wheels, drop towers and water rides for its customers.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SUNDAY: 11:00am-7:00pm

Directions: Click Here


Sanjeevaiah Park

Located by the side of Hussain Sagar Lake, Sanjeevaiah Park enjoys a prime location, at the centre of the city. Named after the former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Damodaram Sanjivayya, this park is maintained by the Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority. A popular tourist spot, this park also has the fame of receiving the Best Open Landscape Award in 2010, under the category of Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage.

TIMINGS : MONDAY - SUNDAY: 8:00am-7:00pm
